ONE TIME GRASSHOPPER

Get ready to flow for your next get-to-together. This all-levels class is great for an office looking to de-stress from the cubicle life or a group who wants a fun way to bond.

You Will: provide the people, the space and sweat. Why not turn your conference room in to a blissed-out studio? Everyone will need a mat, towel and water bottle.

I Will: bring the tunes, the bliss and the good yoga vibes. One week prior to your event I'll ask to send out a survey to your participants to gauge everyone's flow level. No experience is required!

The Details: $150 for a 60 minute session, up to 30 people. Add +$3 for every additional person.

REGULAR ZENMASTER

Practice makes progress in yoga. This weekly class will build on each previous session to take you and your group to new yoga heights (literally and figuratively).

You Will: provide the people, the space and the sweat. A regular group is great but people are welcome to come go. Everyone will need a mat, towel and water bottle.

I Will: bring the tunes, the bliss and the good yoga vibes. Each class will build on the previous to increase everyone's flexibility, strength and general zen. No experience is required!

The Details: $65 for a 60 minute session (4 session minimum on a weekly or bi-weekly basis), up to 30 people. Add +$3 for every additional person.

PRIVATE GURU PRACTICER

Studios can be intimidating. Private classes are a great way to deepen your practice or get your foot in the door (or on the mat) in a more personalized setting.

You Will: provide me a list of goals for your yoga practice and bring a smile, a good attitude and an open mind to our classes. You'll need a mat, towel and water bottle.

I Will: set up a short meeting to make sure we're a good student-teacher match. If we are, I'll structure a class tailored specifically to your needs and goals.

The Details: private class pricing depends on several factors including time, commute and space rental if needed. Please contact me to discuss an exact class quote for your needs.
